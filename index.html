Biography

My name is Katusiime Frank, a 23-year-old Ugandan professional with strong interests in Mechanical Engineering, Software Engineering, Cybersecurity, and Technology. I am also a father of one child and passionate about innovation and continuous learning.

Education Background

I started my education at St. Theresa Primary School.

I later joined Kitebi Secondary School, where I completed both O-Level and A-Level studies.
At A-Level, I studied PCM/GP/ICT (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, General Paper, and Information Technology).

I later pursued Cyber Security and Digital Forensics studies at Kyambogo University, gaining skills in computer systems, cybersecurity, and digital investigations.

Currently, I am studying Mechanical Engineering while also continuing to grow my skills in software development, Linux systems, and cybersecurity.

Skills & Interests
